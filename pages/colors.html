Colors

Our color system is designed to create a cohesive and visually appealing experience throughout our website. The primary and neutral palettes are adaptations of our brand palette, specifically tailored for digital interfaces. We use a clear naming convention for CSS variables to ensure consistency and ease of use. The naming convention follows a pattern of --color-[shade]-[variant], where [shade] represents the color shade (e.g., primary, secondary, ltgrey, dkgrey) and [variant] denotes the specific variant number (e.g., 1, 2).

Primary Web Palette

The primary web palette is the cornerstone of our digital design, providing the essential colors that define our brand's online presence. These colors are used for the most prominent elements to create a strong visual impact.

#a91523
#1125a9

Use case examples:

  • Primary Color: Used for CTA buttons, background color floods, and other prominent elements.
  • Secondary Color: Applied to non-CTA buttons, links, icon sections, and other secondary elements.
  • Accent Color: Utilized sparingly, primarily for hero button hover states and other attention-grabbing elements.
CSS

:root {

--color-primary: #a91523;

--color-secondary: #1125a9;

--color-accent: #fec830;

}
